In order to be ready, here is a list of school requirements so that our children will be ready for the best learning experience.

School Admission Requirements

  • — Completed application form
  • — Copy of your child's birth certificate
  • — 2 passport size photographs of your child
  • — Previous school report (if transferring)
  • — Photocopy of National ID of parent/guardian
  • — Immunization records
