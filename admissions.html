How to Enroll Your Child

We're excited that you're considering Loving Hands Academy for your child's education. Our enrollment process is straightforward and we're here to help you every step of the way.

1

Download the Form

Get our application form from the download button below. You can fill it out on your computer or print it and complete it by hand.

2

Complete the Application

Fill in your child's information, your contact details, and any other required information. Take your time to ensure everything is accurate.

3

Visit Our Office

Bring your completed form and required documents to our school. We'll review everything together and answer any questions you have.
