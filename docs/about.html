About Us

Discover Our Story, Mission & Values

Our Story

Loving Hands Academy is founded on Christian principles and built to nurture the mind, body, and spirit of every learner. We believe that education is more than academics — it's the shaping of character, values, and lifelong purpose.

Our school provides a warm, faith-driven environment where children can explore their talents, grow in confidence, and learn to love God and others.

Directors' Message

At Loving Hands Academy, every child is seen, known, and loved. We're not just raising scholars — we're nurturing kind, resilient, and confident individuals who will influence their world with wisdom and grace. Our commitment is to walk with each child on their learning journey and provide the foundation for a meaningful future.

— The Directors

Our Motto

Our Slogan

Our Mission

To nurture a holistic child—academically, spiritually, and socially—grounded in Biblical truth and transformational character.

Our Vision

To become an academic center of excellence for learning, development, and growth of happy, healthy children.

Our Core Values

Love & Care

We nurture with compassion, ensuring every child feels seen, valued, and supported in their learning journey.

Integrity

We teach our learners to be honest, trustworthy, and responsible — both in school and in life.

Excellence

We strive for the best in everything we do, encouraging children to discover their strengths and reach their full potential.

Faith

Our teaching is rooted in Biblical truth — guiding children to build strong moral and spiritual foundations.

Respect

We celebrate diversity, encourage kindness, and teach our learners to value others as they wish to be valued.

Community

We work hand-in-hand with parents, teachers, and students to build a united, supportive school family.
