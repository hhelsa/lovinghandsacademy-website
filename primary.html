Primary School

Building Strong Foundations · Primary 1-5

Excellence in Elementary Education

Our primary school program builds upon the foundation established in kindergarten, providing comprehensive education that develops academic excellence, critical thinking, character, and leadership skills. We prepare students not just for exams, but for life.

Shaping Future Leaders

At Loving Hands Academy, our primary program goes beyond textbooks and tests. We create well-rounded students who excel academically while developing strong character, leadership abilities, and a genuine love for learning.

Our experienced teachers use engaging, student-centered methods that encourage critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. With small class sizes, we provide individual attention that helps each child reach their full potential.

Our Comprehensive Curriculum

A well-balanced program that develops the whole child

Language & Literacy

Strong emphasis on English language skills, reading comprehension, creative writing, grammar, and effective communication through stories, debates, and presentations.

Mathematics

Comprehensive math instruction covering arithmetic, problem-solving, logical reasoning, and practical applications that build confidence and numerical fluency.

Science & Discovery

Hands-on science education exploring the natural world, conducting experiments, and developing observation skills and scientific thinking.

Social Studies

Understanding our world, culture, geography, history, and developing awareness of Ugandan heritage and global citizenship.

Christian Education

Biblical studies, character development, moral values, and spiritual growth integrated throughout the curriculum and daily life.

Arts & Physical Education

Music, art, drama, and sports programs that develop creativity, physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy lifestyle habits.

Why Choose Our Primary Program

Small Class Sizes

Individual attention with low student-teacher ratios ensures every child gets the support they need to excel.

Experienced Teachers

Our qualified, passionate educators bring years of experience and genuine care for each student's success.

Holistic Development

We develop the whole child—academically, spiritually, physically, socially, and emotionally.
